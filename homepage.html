About us

MedSearch will be a one stop online platform for all information related to medical procedures. We believe this idea will be the next internet-technology based transformational idea in the field of healthcare.

Presently there is no platform that provides medical procedure related information to patients.Patients select a hospital/doctor for a medical procedure based on the feedback from their family doctor/personal network. When we buy a mobile, we go through the ratings, feedback etc on multiple websites, but when we select a doctor or a hospital for a medical procedure, we make this decision in a sub-optimal way, based on unorganized information. We aim to change it via MedSearch.
